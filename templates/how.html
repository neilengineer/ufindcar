How does ufindcar.com work?

What You Do

1) Go to ufindcar.com,spot the best deal.
2) Move mouse over & follow the link appeared!

*Advanced options to select data source, number of cars to show, and a lot of filters available on the left sidebar to help you.

What We do

1) Collect valuable data from the Web.
2) Validate, process and save the data.
3) Display the selected ones.

Ranges

When collecting data, we have following ranges:
*) Price range: < 30000 USD
*) Mileage range:< 150000 miles
*) Model year: > Year 2000
*) Available date: < 2 days
*Only supports San Francisco bay area (for now)

Filter And Display

When displaying the car information collected, the datum will be sorted by price and mileage.

By default, the lowest(top) 100 cars will be displayed. You can specify how many cars to show from the left toolbar.
